Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements for self-drive?

A valid National ID/Passport, a valid Driving License (min 2 years experience), and a refundable security deposit.

Do you offer chauffeur-driven services?

Yes. Professional drivers are available for KSh 2,500/day (Nairobi) and KSh 3,500/day (Outside Nairobi).

Terms & Conditions

1. Rental Duration

Standard rentals are charged per 24-hour cycle. Any excess time over 2 hours is charged as a full day.

2. Fuel Policy

Cars are provided with a full tank and must be returned with a full tank. Otherwise, a 15% refueling surcharge applies.
