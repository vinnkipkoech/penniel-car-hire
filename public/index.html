Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about renting with Penniel.

What do I need to rent a car?

You need a valid National ID or Passport, a valid Driving License (held for at least 2 years), and a 50% deposit to secure your booking.

Is insurance included?

Yes, all our vehicles come with Comprehensive Insurance. However, the hirer is liable for a standard excess fee in case of minor damages or accidental claims.

Do you offer chauffeur-driven services?

Yes. Professional drivers are available for KSh 2,500/day within Nairobi and KSh 3,500/day for trips outside the city.

Terms & Conditions

1. Rental Requirements

Original ID/Passport and a valid Driving License must be presented. A refundable security deposit is mandatory for all self-drive rentals.

2. Usage & Limits

Our vehicles are fitted with GPS trackers. Off-road driving is prohibited unless the vehicle is a 4x4 (e.g., Land Cruiser or Prado).

3. Fuel & Cleaning

Return the car with the same fuel level as received. Smoking and pets are strictly prohibited inside the vehicles.

4. Breakdown & Accidents

In case of any incident, contact our 24/7 emergency line immediately. Do not attempt private repairs without authorization.
